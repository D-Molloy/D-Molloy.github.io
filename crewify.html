Crewify was built for a collaborator's employer, who where still using an inefficient paper-based job system. This React app helps crew-based businesses manage and streamline their job workflow. Managers can create and search jobs to check completion status as well as performance metrics. Crews can access their jobs, utilize navigation and phone links, and enter job details and mark jobs complete.
